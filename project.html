ABOUT US

About Us



In 2016, Our Founder – Sravan, during an international trip visited a Burger restaurant which had multiple burger options & bold flavours like PB&J, classic cheese burger which were native to the country. That’s when the idea clicked – why can’t India has its own burger chain which sells varieties of burgers with unique and varied flavours that every Indian can relate to. That’s when FOODIE was born!


OUR GROWTH.



FOODIE is one of the fastest growing modern casual dining chain of cafes by sravan that exists to delight passionate American cuisine lovers. Walk in and place your order and sit back and relax and soak in our modern to the age ambience as you wait for your order to be freshly prepared. TBC Offers a unique Desi twist to American cuisine that every Indian can relate to. All our food is 100% made to order 100% fresh that tantalise the taste buds and excite your senses. The FOODIE, India is marching towards 100+ opened stores by the end of 2022 and be India’s largest homegrown FOODIE by Dec 2023.
